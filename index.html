SAZZAD HOSSEN EMON

Sazzad Hossen is a Bangladeshi actor works in Bangladesh film industries. Born in 5th of March 1989 in Chittagong city of Bangladesh. He started his career as a Model. He had always wanted to be an actor since his childhood. However, during his University days he felt that the idea of acting was far-fetched and focused on creative writing. While pursuing Bachelor of Science degree from City University of London (UK), Hossain again became interested in acting and after coming back to Bangladesh. And he started auditioning for Men's Fair and Lovely channel i Hero "Ke Hobe Masud Rana?." And he became the 1st runner up in that reality show. In Early life he worked for theater in Bangladesh Udichi Shilpi Gosty (UK), and also worked Zilla Shilpokala Acadamy..

Biography

A biography is simply the story of a real person’s life. It could be about a person who is still alive, someone who lived centuries ago, someone who is globally famous, an unsung hero forgotten by history, or even a unique group of people. The facts of their life, from birth to death (or the present day of the author), are included with life-changing moments often taking center stage. The author usually points to the subject’s childhood, coming-of-age events, relationships, failures, and successes in order to create a well-rounded description of her subject.
